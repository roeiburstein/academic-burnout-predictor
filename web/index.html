⚠️ Ethical Demonstration & Disclaimer This tool is a research and educational demonstration showcasing a tuned binary Logistic Regression classifier. It estimates the likelihood that a student's profile falls into the "High Burnout" category based on synthetic population-level statistical distributions. This is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace psychiatric or professional mental health evaluations.
